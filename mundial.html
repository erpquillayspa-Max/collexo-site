Tournament Format · 2026
Group Stage (Jun 11–27): 48 teams in 12 groups of 4. Each team plays 3 matches. Top 2 from each group advance automatically (24 teams). The 8 best third-placed teams also advance = 32 teams total in the knockout stage.
🆕 New for 2026 — Round of 32: First time in World Cup history. Replaces the old Round of 16 as the first knockout stage. 32 teams, single elimination. Lose = go home. No second chances.
