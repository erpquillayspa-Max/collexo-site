Policies

How we
operate

Collexo commission

Free users: 20% commission on each transaction, min $0.50. Premium users: payment processor cost + 0.5% Collexo fee, min $0.50.

What the commission covers
  • Escrow (money custody) system
  • Dispute resolution by our team
  • Payment processing costs
  • Reputation and verification system
Free trades (no money)

Pure sticker-for-sticker exchanges between Premium users are commission-free. Both parties coordinate shipping and confirm receipt in the app.

How escrow works

When a buyer pays, money is held by Collexo. The seller has 5 business days to ship and upload proof.

When money is released
  • Buyer confirms receipt → immediate release
  • Buyer doesn't confirm within 72h of estimated delivery → automatic release
  • Seller doesn't ship in 5 days → automatic refund to buyer
Open disputes

If a dispute is opened, money is held until resolved, max 7 business days.

How to open a dispute

Buyer can open a dispute within 72 hours of receiving the sticker (or before confirming). Must upload photo or evidence of the issue.

Valid dispute reasons
  • Received sticker doesn't match the listing
  • Sticker arrived damaged
  • Nothing received within the agreed timeframe
  • Seller didn't respond to chat
Sanctions for fraud

Confirmed fraud results in permanent ban. More than 2 disputes in 30 days triggers automatic preventive suspension.

Monthly Premium

Monthly billing. First 3 days are free; cancel before and no charge.

World Cup Pass

One-time payment covering Jun–Dec 2026 (7 months). 40% OFF vs monthly ($18.90 vs $31.50).

Early Bird pre-order

We securely save your card details without charging anything until launch day. You'll be notified 48 hours before. The Early Bird 55% OFF applies to the 1st month only (monthly) or the full pass (World Cup).

⚠️ If you cancel and want to return, you pay the current price at that time.
Cancellation

Status — pending legal review

Proposed model
